# Stability Metrics

Default thresholds:

- Stand stable seconds: at least 30.
- Flat walk: no fall.
- Max body roll: at most 8 degrees.
- Max body pitch: at most 8 degrees.
- Foot slip ratio: at most 0.12.
- Joint torque margin: at least 20%.
- Average speed: at least 0.5 m/s.
- Cost of transport: at most 2.5.

Scoring weights:

- IK: 10
- Phase: 10
- Stand: 15
- Flat walk: 20
- Posture: 15
- Slip: 10
- Torque margin: 10
- Speed/energy: 10
