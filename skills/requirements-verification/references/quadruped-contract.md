# Quadruped MVP Contract

The minimum robot-dog contract should cover:

- mass and payload
- runtime and battery voltage
- target flat-ground walking speed
- standing stability duration
- joint torque margin
- maximum body dimensions
- emergency stop and surface-temperature safety
- manufacturing process and primary material

The matching verification matrix should include at least:

- mechanical interference check
- mass and center-of-mass check
- PCB connector and support check
- ERC/DRC and power budget check
- standing stability check
- flat-walk gait check
- manufacturability check
